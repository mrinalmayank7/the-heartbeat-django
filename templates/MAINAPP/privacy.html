Data we collect for prediction / diagnostic tools

Diabetes Prediction

The diabetes risk prediction tool is provided you with eight input fields which is used for diagnosing the presence of diabetes. The date we collect in order to process the outcome are Pregnancies ,Glucose ,Blood Pressure, Skin Thickness ,Insulin ,BMI , Diabetes Pedigree Function & Age.


Cardiovascular Disease Prediction

The Cardiovascular Disease prediction tool is provided along with eleven input fields to predict the target outcome on the basis of the measures provided. The date we collect in order to process the outcome are Age, Resting blood pressure, Cholesterol, Thalach, Oldpeak, Gender, Fasting blood sugar, Chest pain type, Resting electrocardiographic, Exercise induced angina, Slope of the peak exercise ST segment.


Liver Disease Prediction

The date we collect in order to process the outcome are Age, Gender, Total Bilirubin, Direct Bilirubin, Alkaline Phosphotase, Alamine Aminotransferase, Aspartate Aminotransferase, Total Proteins, Albumin, Albumin and Globulin Ratio.


Brain Tumour Prediction

The date we collect in order to process the outcome are Mean, Variance, Standard Deviation, Entropy, Skewness, Kurtosis, Contrast, Energy, ASM, Homogeneity, Dissimilarity, Correlation, Coarseness, PSNR, SSIM, MSE, DC.
